Pinball Quizzards FAQ

Q: Why are you called the Pinball Quizzards?

A: Because there's pinball in the bar. And because we do pub trivia. And "wizzard" with two z's is a Discworld reference? Ask Mia Kiyashka.

Q: Why do you always come in second place?

A: Historically, and by tradition, the Quizzards fall down in the music round, which is always the penultimate round.

Q: Can I bring food into the bar?

A: Yes.

Q: What makes you say Solid State is a hipster bar?

A: The owner wears a white cowboy hat. I rest my case.

Q: Tell me of the mysterious Danny Friendly, host of pub trivia at Solid State.

A: That's not in the form of a question, but I'll respond. We know little of Danny Friendly, and the little we do know surfaces yet more questions. Danny Friendly has let slip he knows quizmaster Johnny Goodtimes, the originator of Quizzo. I entreat you not to pursue this feckless quest for forbidden knowledge, but—no—I see that steely glint of determination in your eye. Seek out this Johnny Goodtimes if you dare, and godspeed. But I warn you...do not face Danny Friendly without learning his secret vulnerability, lest you doom us all.

Q: How did you make this bot?

A: It's a Python script that runs on my laptop. If my laptop is off, no reminder that week. You can see the (pretty sloppy) code here.
